# Claim Boundary

Nyx claims:

- a local-first demo can present weak evidence as a conservative action card;
- route confidence can be separated from absolute position;
- action text can stay short under pressure;
- a UI can refuse to over-confirm.

Nyx does not claim:

- operational readiness;
- safety certification;
- person, threat, hazard, or weapon identification;
- entry clearance;
- targeting, engagement, or autonomous control;
- a complete sensor stack;
- a military rule engine.

The sample data is synthetic and intentionally incomplete.
